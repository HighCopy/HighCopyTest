Summary

This game is about the battle for dominance between Bio Mechanical Invasion BMI and Phantoms

Story

Phantoms trying to control this location by sending team from red roar to rule this caves and BMI will not surrender and phantoms let them make civilian evacuation

Background

Moon City is a new city too close to a caves full of resources and anyone control of this city will ruling this location

BMI it is a company specialized in renting mutant soldiers or selling rare and mutant animal

Phantoms it is a company specialized in selling weapons and providing various services such as renting a number of soldiers or private security personnel, as well as heavy equipment.
