History Of Monsters

Monsters Capital

The capital of the Kingdom of Monsters is distinguished by the beauty of the quiet nature, the vast spaces, and the art of architecture integrated with the environment. There are many combat training centers, and in the middle of the city is the Pyramid of Light, one of the largest buildings used as a giant generator of energy, as well as a giant library and a distinguished university, as well as treatment centers specialized in chemotherapy. And the associated industries, as well as chemical laboratories, genetic development laboratories for humans and animals, places for the development and breeding of animals and beasts, and the main weapons factory for the royal army, as well as its main camp.
