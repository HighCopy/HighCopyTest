Queen Square

It is the central square of the capital of the Kingdom of Sky Wing, connecting the four parts of the capital. In the middle of the square is a green crystal statue similar to the square’s emblem. It is surrounded by a vast area for the people to gather in on occasions, public announcements, and public trials. Behind the square is the building of the Supreme Council of the State and a group of hotels. Spread out in the square and wide public gardens
